Healthcare

We help providers, payers, and life sciences teams combine Generative AI and Enterprise Architecture to improve patient experience, reduce clinician burden, and streamline revenue cycle—while protecting PHI and meeting HIPAA and organizational policy.

Challenges We Address

  • Validating GenAI for clinical and operational use—reducing hallucinations, bias, and drift; enforcing human oversight and audit trails.
  • Integrating AI with EHRs via FHIR/HL7, patient comms, and care management without disrupting clinician workflow.
  • Protecting PHI with data minimization, role-based access, and Zero Trust patterns across hybrid cloud and on-prem systems.
  • Measuring impact on quality, safety, and throughput (CDI, prior auth, denials, coding) with defensible metrics and governance.
